About our principle

Dr. Anjan Roy is the esteemed principal of our college, a visionary leader who has played a pivotal role in transforming our institution. With a strong educational background, Dr. Roy holds a Bachelor of Science (BSc) degree in Computer Science, a Master of Science (MSc) degree in Computer Science, a Master of Technology (MTech) degree in Cloud Computing, and a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) degree in Research for Cloud Computing.

Since joining our college, Dr. Anjan Roy has demonstrated exceptional expertise and a deep passion for his field. His extensive knowledge in cloud computing has paved the way for remarkable advancements in our institution. Under his leadership, the college has witnessed incredible developments and achievements.

Dr. Roy's innovative approach and dedication to excellence have resulted in significant progress in various areas of the college. Through the implementation of cutting-edge technologies and strategic initiatives, he has enhanced the quality of education, research, and infrastructure. These improvements have not gone unnoticed, as the college was honored with the prestigious NAAC Grade A accreditation in 2022.

Thanks to Dr. Anjan Roy's relentless pursuit of excellence and his commitment to providing a conducive learning environment, our college has emerged as a hub of innovation and academic excellence. His transformative leadership has inspired both students and faculty members to strive for greatness, and his vision continues to shape the future of our institution.

Kolkata College is an esteemed institution with a clear mission to provide quality education and foster intellectual growth. Our vision is to be a leading center of excellence in higher education, empowering students to become lifelong learners and responsible global citizens.

Our objectives revolve around academic excellence, holistic development, and community engagement. We strive to offer diverse programs that meet the needs of our students and prepare them for the challenges of the modern world. We aim to create a supportive learning environment that nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and leadership skills.

What sets Kolkata College apart is its distinctiveness. We embrace cultural diversity and promote inclusivity, encouraging students from all backgrounds to thrive and contribute meaningfully to society. Our emphasis on experiential learning, research, and innovation ensures that our graduates are well-equipped to make a positive impact in their chosen fields.

At Kolkata College, we are committed to inspiring and empowering the next generation of leaders, instilling in them a passion for knowledge, a commitment to social responsibility, and a drive for personal and professional growth.
